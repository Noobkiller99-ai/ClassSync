Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Class Sync is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to sync your schedule to Google Calendar:

  • TCS iON Credentials: Your TCS iON email and password are encrypted at rest on our server. They are only used to authenticate and fetch your timetable events from the TCS iON portal.
  • Google OAuth Token: When you connect your Google Calendar, we store the OAuth access and refresh tokens returned by Google. These are used solely to access, update, and manage the specific calendar you authorize for Class Sync.
  • Timetable Data: We temporarily cache your upcoming timetable events to enable calendar syncing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide and maintain the synchronization service:

  • To securely retrieve your timetable from TCS iON.
  • To create and update calendar events in your authorized Google Calendar.
  • To run background refresh updates weekly (if credentials are saved).

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your credentials and data:

  • Credentials are encrypted at rest using cryptography libraries before database storage.
  • Data transfers are carried out exclusively over secure HTTPS channels.
  • We never sell, rent, or share your credentials or timetable data with any third parties.

4. User Rights and Deletion

You have full control over your data. Clicking Sign out or Disconnect inside the Class Sync application will instantly and permanently delete all your stored credentials, tokens, and cached timetable events from our database.

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at asishsatpathy99@gmail.com.
